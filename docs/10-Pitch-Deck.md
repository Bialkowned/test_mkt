## Presentation Tips

### Do's
‚úÖ Keep slides visual (minimal text)  
‚úÖ Tell stories, not just facts  
‚úÖ Show passion and conviction  
‚úÖ Know your numbers cold  
‚úÖ Practice demo thoroughly  
‚úÖ Anticipate objections  
‚úÖ Be honest about challenges  

### Don'ts
‚ùå Read from slides  
‚ùå Over-explain obvious things  
‚ùå Get defensive about competition  
‚ùå Make unsupported claims  
‚ùå Rush through important points  
‚ùå Ignore questions  

### Timing
- **Slides 1-3**: 3 minutes (Problem)
- **Slides 4-5**: 3 minutes (Solution + Market)
- **Slides 6-8**: 4 minutes (Business Model + Traction + Competition)
- **Slides 9-11**: 3 minutes (GTM + Financials + Team)
- **Slide 12-13**: 2 minutes (Ask + Vision)
- **Q&A**: 10-15 minutes

**Total**: 15 minutes + Q&A
